The Open Air, The Fresh Water and the Filthy Muck

The Bad Fomo Adventure Park the place we named wrong on purpose and sometimes say right by accident! Perfect for birthday gatherings and work retreats, Stag and Hen parties. Whatever excites the group. Team building exercises, competitive rock climbing or a casual weekend inside the outdoors! We have something for you.

Cosy 5 Star Cabins, Glamping or Camp in the Valley

Whether it's luxury you are after or you want to get down and dirty in nature or perhaps somewhere in between then we have a spot for you. Our campsite is fully equipped with hot showers, a power bank station (because phone is......my......precious) or if you would prefer leave your phone charging in a locker in the hut so that technology can be left behind while you Kumbaya into the night!

5 Star Restaurant, Communal Kitchen or BBQ

At the end of the day you'll have worked up an appetite so we have all kinds of options to match the experience that you have booked for yourself. there are plenty of activities in the evening time so occupy your mind should you choose to avoid a goodnights sleep with a year round carnival and fair to entertain the kids or a more adult experience with a bar and music late into the night
